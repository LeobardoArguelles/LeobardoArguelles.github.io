Add the following content to ~/etc/X11/xorg.conf.d/00-keyboard.conf:

```text
Section "InputClass"
    Identifier             "Keyboard Defaults"
    MatchIsKeyboard       "yes"
    Option                 "XkbLayout" "us"
    Option                 "XkbVariant" "intl"
EndSection
```

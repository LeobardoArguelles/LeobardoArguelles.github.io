Suponga \\(\exists\ A^{-1}\\) y dado \\(Ax=b\ \therefore \ x=A^{-1}b\\)

Comprobación directa:

\begin{align\*}
P\_1&: Ax = b\\\\
P\_2&: A^{-1}\\\\
P\_3&: A^{-1}Ax = A^{-1}b\\\\
P\_4&: AA^{-1} = I\\\\
P\_5&: Ix = A^{-1}b\\\\
P\_6&: Ix = x\\\\
Entonces&:\ x = A^{-1}b\\\\
\end{align\*}

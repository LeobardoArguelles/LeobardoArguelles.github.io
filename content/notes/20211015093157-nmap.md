## BASIC {#basic}

TCM recommended command:

```shell
nmap -T4 -p- -A <ip>
```

Where:

-   T4 is the speeed from 1 (slow) to 5 (fast)
-   -p- asks to scan every port.
-   -A enables scripts to get more info


## SMB {#smb}

```shell
nmap -p445,139 --scrit smb-protocols <ip>
```
